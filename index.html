About Us

NailsByTee
Where Art Meets Elegance

NailsByTee was founded by the hardworking and passionate Tee Masimula, a dedicated nail artist who turned her dream into reality. With an eye for detail and a heart full of creativity, Tee has built a sanctuary where every client leaves feeling beautiful and confident.

Located in the vibrant community of Sibiya Stand, Blaaubosch at Theku Mall Road, NailsByTee offers a warm, welcoming space where precision meets artistry.
